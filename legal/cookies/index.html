Cookies Policy

Effective Date: May 25, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how MissionPeak ("MissionPeak", "Siddhatek", "we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website, use our mobile application, or interact with our Services.

Essential cookies are necessary for the Service to function and are placed automatically. Non-essential cookies — including analytics, functionality, and advertising cookies — are only activated after you provide consent through our cookie banner. You may manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser, device settings, or in-app privacy controls.

1. What Are Cookies?​

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website or use an app. They help us recognize your device, remember your preferences, and improve your experience.

MissionPeak may also use related technologies such as:

  • Web beacons
  • Pixels
  • Local storage
  • SDK-based mobile tracking identifiers

These technologies function similarly to cookies.

2. Types of Cookies We Use​

MissionPeak uses the following categories of cookies:

2.1 Essential Cookies​

These cookies are necessary for the operation of our Services. They enable:

  • Account login and authentication
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Core app functionality (e.g., messaging, group participation)

You cannot disable essential cookies.

2.2 Performance & Analytics Cookies​

These cookies help us understand how users interact with MissionPeak so we can improve the experience. They may collect:

  • App usage patterns
  • Feature engagement
  • Crash reports
  • Device and performance metrics

Examples include analytics tools such as:

  • Google Analytics
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Mixpanel (if used)

2.3 Functionality Cookies​

These cookies allow MissionPeak to remember your preferences, such as:

  • Saved settings
  • Location preferences
  • Notification choices
  • Language or region

They enhance personalization but are not strictly required.

2.4 Advertising & Marketing Cookies (If Applicable)​

If MissionPeak uses advertising or promotional tools, these cookies may:

  • Deliver relevant content
  • Measure campaign effectiveness
  • Prevent repetitive ads

MissionPeak uses first-party targeted advertising only. Ads are selected internally by MissionPeak based on your in-app activity preferences and interests — no personal information is transmitted to advertisers. MissionPeak does not use third-party advertising cookies, cross-app tracking technologies, or advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID). Advertisers receive only aggregated performance metrics such as total impressions and clicks. You may opt out of first-party ad targeting at any time under Settings > Privacy > Advertising Preferences.

2.5 Location-Based Cookies​

If you enable location services, we may use cookies or device identifiers to:

  • Suggest nearby groups or events
  • Improve outdoor activity recommendations
  • Enhance safety and community relevance

You can disable location access at any time through device settings.

3. How We Use Cookies​

MissionPeak uses cookies to:

  • Authenticate users and maintain sessions
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Personalize content and recommendations
  • Analyze usage trends
  • Enhance safety and prevent misuse
  • Provide customer support and diagnostics

Cookies help us deliver a smoother, more relevant experience.

4. Third-Party Cookies​

Some cookies are placed by third-party partners that help us operate MissionPeak, such as:

  • Analytics providers
  • Cloud hosting services
  • Payment processors (if applicable)
  • Social login providers (Google, Apple, etc.)

These partners may collect information about your device or interactions with MissionPeak. They are required to process data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

5. How to Manage or Disable Cookies​

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser settings (for website use)
  • Mobile device settings (for app use)
  • In-app privacy controls (if provided)

You may:

  • Block cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Limit tracking
  • Disable advertising identifiers

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect functionality or limit your ability to use some features.

6. Do Not Track Signals​

MissionPeak does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals due to lack of industry-wide standards. We will update this policy if standards emerge.

7. Changes to This Cookie Policy​

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date and notify you as required by law.

Continued use of MissionPeak after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us​

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy, you may contact us at:

MissionPeak Support Email: support@siddhatek.com
