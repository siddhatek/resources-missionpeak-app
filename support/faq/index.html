Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about using MissionPeak.

General​

MissionPeak is a dynamic platform designed to connect and inspire outdoor enthusiasts. Our mission is to empower individuals and communities by offering tools that promote adventure, wellness, and meaningful connections. With MissionPeak, you can discover a wide range of outdoor events, join vibrant communities, track your fitness goals, and draw inspiration from seasoned explorers.

MissionPeak is for everyone — from experienced outdoor aficionados to those new to the world of exploration. The platform provides resources and communities that cater to all skill levels, ensuring that you can find events and groups that match your adventure style and personal goals.

Getting started is easy! Download the MissionPeak app from your device's app store, sign up with your email or social media account, and personalize your profile. You can immediately begin exploring events, joining communities, and tracking your outdoor activities.

MissionPeak is available as a mobile app for iOS and Android. Some features, like browsing public content, may also be accessible from a web browser. Check your device's app store for the latest version and supported operating system requirements.

You can view some general, public information without an account, but creating a free account is required to join Huddles, participate in Communities, track activities, or publish Inspirations. Signing up only takes a couple of minutes with an email address or a supported social login.

While non-members can view general information about MissionPeak, access to detailed event information, participation, and community features typically requires creating an account. This ensures a secure and personalized experience for all users.

Features​

The MissionPeak app features a user-friendly interface where you can browse and filter events based on your preferences, location, and interests. Whether you're interested in hiking, camping, or other outdoor activities, the app offers a comprehensive list of events that you can join. You can also receive personalized recommendations tailored to your past activities and preferences.

A Huddle is a single planned activity — like a Saturday hike or a one-time meetup — with its own date, location, and participant list. A Community is an ongoing group built around a shared interest, where members can post updates, share Inspirations, and organize multiple Huddles over time. Think of a Community as the group, and a Huddle as one event that group might run.

Our app enables users to connect with like-minded individuals by joining or creating communities. You can participate in group discussions, share photos and stories, and organize events with peers who share your passion for the outdoors. These communities foster collaboration and allow you to build friendships based on shared interests and outdoor experiences.

MissionPeak integrates cutting-edge fitness tracking tools that help you monitor your activity levels and achievements. Track metrics like distance hiked, elevation gained, calories burned, and more. You can set personal goals, view progress reports, and celebrate milestones, all within the app. This feature helps you stay motivated and engaged on your fitness journey.

Core features like Huddles, Communities, and the Newsfeed require an internet connection to stay in sync. If you're heading somewhere with limited signal, it's a good idea to download any trail details or maps you need in advance and double-check meeting details before you lose connectivity.

Within the app, you'll find curated content from renowned trailblazers and influencers who share their experiences, tips, and breathtaking stories from their outdoor adventures. These narratives are designed to motivate you, provide practical advice, and introduce you to new ways of exploring the natural world.

Yes, you can organize and manage your own events within the MissionPeak app. Whether it's a group hike, camping trip, or a community meetup, you can create an event, set the details, and invite other members to join. This feature helps to bring together fellow adventurers and foster strong community bonds.

Users can share various content types including photos, videos, and written posts about their outdoor experiences. Whether you're documenting your latest hike or sharing tips on gear, MissionPeak provides a platform for creative expression and knowledge sharing among enthusiasts.

MissionPeak may offer features like virtual badges, challenges, and leaderboards to encourage sharing and enhance user engagement. By participating in these features, you can earn recognition within the community and motivate others by showcasing your accomplishments.

The MissionPeak app periodically hosts challenges and competitions that you can join. Simply access the "Challenges" section in the app to find current opportunities, register for those that interest you, and track your progress directly within the app.

Account & Privacy​

MissionPeak is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. We use robust security protocols to safeguard user information and comply with applicable data protection laws. You can find detailed information in our Privacy Policy, accessible via the app and our website.

You can update your profile details, email, and password at any time from the Settings menu in the app. If you signed up using a social login (Google, Facebook, or Apple), password changes are managed through that provider instead.

See Changing Your Username or Date of Birth for details.

Billing & Subscriptions​

MissionPeak offers a range of services for free, allowing you to explore many of its features without any cost. For those seeking an enhanced experience, premium memberships may be available which offer additional benefits such as exclusive events, advanced tracking features, and more personalized content. Check the app for specific details on pricing and features.

A free account gives you access to the core MissionPeak experience — joining Huddles and Communities, tracking activities, and following Connections. Premium memberships, where available, may add benefits like advanced tracking, exclusive events, or additional personalization. Specific premium features and pricing are shown in the app, since they may vary by region or change over time.

Subscriptions are managed through the app store you subscribed through (Apple App Store or Google Play). Open your device's subscription settings to view, change, or cancel a MissionPeak subscription. If you need help locating it, our Support Center can point you in the right direction.

Support & Safety​

Our dedicated support team is ready to assist you with any app-related questions or concerns. Access the "Contact Us" section in the app to reach our support center, where you can find FAQs, troubleshooting guides, or submit a request for direct assistance.

Safety is a top priority for MissionPeak. While the app provides information about events, each user is encouraged to follow safety guidelines and prepare accordingly. We offer resources and tips on safe practices in the outdoors, and we encourage event organizers to include safety information in their event descriptions.

If you come across content or behavior that violates our community standards, use the report option available on the post, profile, Huddle, or Community in question. Our team reviews reports and takes action in line with our Acceptable Use Policy.

Business & Partnerships​

MissionPeak collaborates with outdoor influencers who provide expert insights, tips, and motivational content. These partnerships are meant to enrich the user's experience by providing authentic, value-driven content that resonates with the outdoor community.

Businesses can collaborate with MissionPeak by sponsoring events, offering products for community challenges, or employing advertising within the app. Interested businesses can contact our partnership team through the "Business Solutions" section on our website or app to explore opportunities.
